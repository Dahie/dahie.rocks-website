About

Dahie is singer and instrumentalist Daniel Senff based in Vienna. Usually starting with a single Ukulele he weaves songs that grow in layers of sound and depths. Not danceable, laid back, sometimes dreamy, sometimes nightmarish.

On stage he's a rare sight, in a ligher tone and is usually bringing only his Ukulele.

… if he can remember the lyrics.
